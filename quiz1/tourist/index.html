Tourist Place in Samarinda

Samarinda is home to a variety of captivating tourist attractions that showcase the citys rich culture and natural beauty. One of the most iconic sites is the Mahakam River, where visitors can take scenic boat tours to admire the stunning landscapes and traditional villages along the banks. The Samarinda Botanical Garden offers a peaceful retreat, featuring diverse plant species native to Borneo, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and families. For those interested in culture, the Islamic Center of Samarinda stands out with its impressive architecture and serene ambiance, providing insight into the local Islamic heritage. Adventure seekers can explore the Mount Mesangat area, known for its hiking trails and breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. Additionally, the vibrant Samarinda Night Market is a must-visit, where visitors can indulge in local delicacies and experience the lively atmosphere of the city..
